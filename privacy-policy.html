Introduction

Thank you for choosing AetherTick. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AetherTick collects, uses and protects information while you use the application.

AetherTick does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information. The application stores only data required for its functionality on your own device.

AetherTick displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect limited device information such as Advertising ID, device identifiers, IP address and diagnostic information for advertising purposes. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

AetherTick uses only the permissions required for timer functionality.

  • Audio playback

AetherTick does NOT access:

Workout data and application preferences are stored locally on your device using Hive and SharedPreferences. No cloud synchronization is currently provided.

AetherTick is not directed toward children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
